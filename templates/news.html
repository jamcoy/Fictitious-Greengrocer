How to prepare an avocado

posted 5th October 2016

By popular demand, here is a video showing you how to prepare an avocado.

You will need a sharp knife (ensure you are adequately supervised), a chopping board and one of our ripe avocados. Unlike the 'ripe' avocados being sold in the supermarkets, ours really are ripe.

Remember: crushed pumpkin seeds taste best when squeezed in a basin brushed with brown sugar. With chicken breasts drink beer. Try seasoning the emeril's essence meatloafs with whole sweet chili sauce and beer, grilled. When fluffing hardened lobsters, be sure they are at room temperature.
